Salesforce just made Shopper Agent, Buyer Agent, and Merchant Agent generally available — and native in ChatGPT and Gemini.

The headline is "agentic commerce." The reality is more interesting.

Salesforce is the first mega-vendor to build agents that act on real data — inventory, pricing, order cutoffs — not just chat. Shopper Agent checks stock and closes the sale. Buyer Agent procures via WhatsApp. Merchant Agent lets your team run catalog ops in plain language.

This is the "harness over model" thesis playing out at enterprise scale. Salesforce doesn't have the best foundation model. But they own the data layer, the permissions, and the workflow.

For every startup building a shopping chatbot: the gap isn't the LLM. It's whether your agent can promise a real delivery date against actual inventory.

That's not a model problem. That's a data-integration-and-governance problem.
