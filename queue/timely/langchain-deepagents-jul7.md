LangChain just released DeepAgents — an open-source, model-agnostic agent harness.

I've been saying the model is not your bottleneck, the harness is. Harrison just built the biggest validation of that thesis from the biggest agent framework company.

Model-agnostic matters more than most people realize. If you pin your agent to one model, you're one API pricing change away from rebuilding your entire stack. A good harness makes models interchangeable — and that's the difference between a fragile prototype and something you'd bet a business on.

The take I'd extend further: the best harness doesn't just abstract the model. It abstracts the tools, the memory, and the governance layer too. That's where the real leverage lives.
