The White House accuses Moonshot of distilling Anthropic's Fable to build Kimi K3.

Same week Hugging Face had to use GLM-5.2 (Chinese open-weight) to analyze the OpenAI rogue agent breach — because US models' guardrails blocked the job.

The governance posture that keeps US models "safe" also makes them useless for defense. Meanwhile, Chinese models are unrestricted, ship open weights, and increasingly power the forensic tools we rely on.

The "open vs closed" debate frames this as a choice between safety and capability. What it actually describes is a choice between who gets to use the capabilities.

Your threat model is incomplete if it only accounts for who built the model. It needs to account for who can actually deploy it when the incident happens.
