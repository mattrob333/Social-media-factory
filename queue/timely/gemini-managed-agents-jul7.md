Google just dropped background tasks, remote MCP, and free-tier start for Managed Agents in the Gemini API.

This is the right abstraction. The industry spent 18 months competing on who has the best model. Google is competing on who makes agents cheapest to put in production.

Background execution alone kills the "my agent times out waiting for a long task" pattern that kills half the agent demos I see. Remote MCP means your agent can call real tools without you building custom integrations. Free tier means you stop gatekeeping experimentation behind a credit card form.

If you're building agents, today's release matters more than any model benchmark this week. The harness just got cheaper, faster, and more available.
