Masayoshi Son just predicted 100 trillion AI agents by 2040. Making their own decisions. Communicating with other agents.

That's a vision.

But here's the question nobody at SoftBank World asked: who manages the identities of those 100 trillion agents?

Every one of them is a non-human identity with permissions. Every one can act on systems. Every one has a surface area that can be compromised.

The infrastructure vision is grand. The governance vision is a blank page.

We're going to spend $5T/year on compute and energy and discover that the hardest problem isn't powering the agents — it's knowing who approved what they did.
