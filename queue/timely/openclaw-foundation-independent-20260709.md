The alternative is every agent you deploy answering to one cloud terms of service — and that terms page can change without your approval. An MIT-licensed independent foundation is the only structure that survives the current land grab with the user's interests intact.

The agent identity council forming alongside it is the part worth watching. Every agent you run is a non-human identity with permissions and no manager. Getting that on the roadmap before the first public breach means someone on the team already mapped the second-order risk.
