Microsoft's MAI model in Excel matches GPT-5.6 quality on common tasks at lower cost.

They trained it from inside the product stack — started with MAI-Code-1-Flash, then specialized on Excel workflows with RL. Same model, different data, cheaper compute.

This is what I mean when I say the model is not the bottleneck. The harness is.

Microsoft didn't build a bigger model. They built a narrower one, trained on the actual workflow, evaluated on the actual task. And it beat a frontier model on the dimensions that matter to actual users.

The next 1,000 AI winners aren't building better general models. They're taking a good enough base and hill-climbing on their own data, their own workflows, their own evaluations.

The model is the commodity. What you do with it — the harness, the data, the loop — is the product.
