OpenAI's new guide on managing AI investments in the agentic era has five solid steps: track usage, evaluate model ROI, govern workflows, fund what compounds, match capacity to demand.

Good framework. Missing the critical layer.

Step 3 says "govern advanced workflows before they scale" — access, tools, approved actions. That's identity governance without saying the words. Every one of those workflows is a non-human identity with permissions and no manager, running plugins and Computer Use across enterprise systems.

Track cost per accepted outcome all you want. If nobody owns the agent making the decision, you're optimizing spend on something with no audit trail.

Fix the identity layer first. Then optimize the spend.
