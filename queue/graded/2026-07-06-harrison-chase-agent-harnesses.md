Harrison Chase just said the agent industry shifted from frameworks to harnesses. He's right.

The model is not your bottleneck. I've watched frontier models faceplant because the harness fed them garbage, and smaller models win because someone engineered the context.

But the real shift nobody's naming: frameworks gave you vocabulary (chains, tools, prompts). Harnesses give you runtime — identity, permissions, audit, rollback.

That's the difference between a demo and a deployment.
