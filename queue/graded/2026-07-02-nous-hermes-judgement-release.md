The most underrated feature in Hermes Agent v0.18 (the Judgement Release) isn't Mixture-of-Agents or /learn — it's Journey: a running record of why the agent made the call it made.

I run my content ops on Hermes. For a year the thing I wanted wasn't a smarter model, it was a receipt for its decisions.

Every agent you deploy is a non-human identity making calls with no manager. Deploy one without an audit trail like Journey and you're operating blind — that's the compliance gap, not a nice-to-have.
