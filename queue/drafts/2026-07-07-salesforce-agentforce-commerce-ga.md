Salesforce just made Shopper Agent, Buyer Agent, and Merchant Agent generally available — and native in ChatGPT and Gemini.

The headline is "agentic commerce." The real story is plumbing.

Shopper Agent checks live stock and closes the sale. Buyer Agent procures over WhatsApp. Merchant Agent runs catalog ops in plain language. None of that works without Salesforce owning inventory, pricing, and order-cutoff data underneath the chat layer.

Startups building shopping chatbots are chasing the wrong rabbit. The gap isn't the LLM. It's that your bot can't promise a real delivery date because it can't see the customer's actual inventory. Salesforce wins on data access, not on having the best model.

That's a data-integration-and-governance problem, not a model problem.
